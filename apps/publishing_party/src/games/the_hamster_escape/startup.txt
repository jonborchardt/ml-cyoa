You are a tiny hamster living in a cage in your owner’s bedroom. Your cage has a squeaky wheel, a water bottle, a pile of wood shavings, and a little purple hamster ball. Every morning, your owner gives you crunchy food and a few sunflower seeds. You like your cage, but you miss your hamster family.[n/]
Your owner loves you and says to you, “You must be living the life. You have a blue hamster wheel, igloo, and a whole hamster tube maze. He leaves suddenly, accidentally leaving the cage door open.[n/]
You freeze.[n/]
The room looks huge from inside your cage. The floor stretches out like a giant desert, and you know there could be predators out there. Maybe the family cat is hiding under the bed. Maybe the dog is sleeping in the hallway. Maybe there are other dangers you have never even seen before.[n/]
Still, this might be your only chance to find your hamster family.[n/]
You creep toward the open door and look down. Your heart beats fast.[n/]
