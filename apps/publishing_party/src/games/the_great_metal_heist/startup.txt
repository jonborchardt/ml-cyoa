Now your story begins.
You start in a small town as a detective trying to find out who stole a priceless artifact from the museum.
This artifact is a key link to holding the world together. If it is not found, the world could be in danger.
The thief has already escaped, and there is a time limit. There are no other detectives besides you, so it is your job to figure out who the thief is and where the artifact went.
You know you need clues, but you are not sure where to begin.
You have three choices.

*label page3
You sprint as fast as you can.
You arrive at the quiet and cold police station. Policemen are all around, sitting at their desks.
You say, “Officer, officer! There’s a strange man chasing me!”
“Oh really, where is he?” the officer says.
“Follow me!”
“Ok.”
Then you see the man, and you say, “I see him!”
Then the officer sprints at the man and blocks his path.
The man tries to hide, but the officer catches him.
You spill with joy.
The man is arrested.
Then the officer says, “Thanks for telling us.”
“No problem!” you say.
You’re now safe, for now.

*label page6
You decide to stay in the market and wait to see if the thief shows up.
At first, you do not see anything suspicious. The market is crowded with people buying food, jewelry, and supplies.
Then you notice a knocked-over booth. You realize this might be the thief because you see a faint sparkle, and you remember the artifact was sparkly.
You rush through the market after them, but they hurry back into the crowd.
Finally, you stop the thief by knocking over a market booth full of vegetables. They fall onto the ground.
You grab the thief and bring him to jail.
The stolen artifact is returned.
The world is saved, for now.

*finish

You say, “What’s in your pocket?”
“Nothing,” The man says. And then… He pulls out a razor-sharp bayonet, threatening you. You realize it’s a robber! What do you do now?

You decide, “Why not. I can take this guy.” You start a fist fight with the guy, you see a different gold medal, but then you realize it is brass knuckles — golden knuckles that hurt a lot!
In a flash, you get hit right in the nose. Your nose is drowning with blood, and then you start to get nauseous.
Just then, as the man is about to stab you in the chest, a big BOOM!!!
Smoke fills the air. A duel is underway nearby, with duelists armed with revolvers. Henry thinks this is unfair. The duel started because Henry McCallister got less gold than John Evergreen.
You cough up dust. As you stand up, the man drops one of his brass knuckles. You pick up his brass knuckles, but then it was a trap!
He jumps at you with one final blow.
OOF!
