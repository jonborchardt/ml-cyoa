*title The Maze
*author Severn, Michael, Avery & Max S.

You, a boy named Eli, are getting ready for the next school year and are trying to find cool artifacts for your school projects. You’ll be starting middle school and know you’ll need a collection of artifacts for your school projects. You are visiting San Diego, California with your cousins. While exploring with them, they dare you to go into a creepy old house that’s been abandoned for over 500 years! Little do you and your cousins know, there is a maze inside the house. Will you be able to find your way out alive? Good Luck!
