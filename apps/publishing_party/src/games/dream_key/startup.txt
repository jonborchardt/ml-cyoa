*title The Search for the Dream Key
*author Kyaihla, Michaela & William

In this story, you are an agile spy trapped in the monster world after a monster steals the Dream Key. You must explore dangerous places, find the key, and unlock the Dream Gate before your choices lead you into danger. Good Luck.

*label page1
You are an agile spy who solves mysteries with magic powers. You can move quickly, sneak through tight spaces, and escape danger. One day, a monster pulls you through a portal and into the monster dimension.
There are two dimensions: the human world and the monster world. Portals connect the two worlds, and the Dream Key unlocks the Dream Gate between them.
But now you are trapped.
The monster who pulled you into the monster world has stolen the Dream Key, so you cannot get back home. Your goal is to find the monster, get the Dream Key back, and unlock the Dream Gate before it is too late.
The monster dimension is full of monsters, portals, and strange places. Some choices may lead you to danger. Some choices may lead you to a happy ever after. You look around and see three possible paths.
