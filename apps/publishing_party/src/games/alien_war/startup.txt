You are walking to high school. You sit on a bench to look at the view. The sun is shining. The birds are chirping. Nothing could possibly go wrong.
“Hi, Robert,” your neighbor says.
“Hi,” you say back.
Suddenly, from out of the blue, you hear a loud noise. A UFO crashes into the Statue of Liberty. Rubble rains down on you, trapping your leg. You cannot see anything. People are running around, screaming and yelling.
You try pulling on your leg, and to your credit, it works. Your leg breaks free. You get up and take in your surroundings.
The Statue of Liberty is surrounded by smoke and broken pieces of stone. UFOs zoom across the sky. Then you notice a bunch of flyers flying around in the air. You grab three of them.
The first flyer says, Come help blow up the mothership!
The second flyer says, Negotiate with the aliens. Get sucked up into the tractor beam and board the mothership!
The third flyer says, Fight with the superheroes to destroy the UFOs! You need to make a decision.

You get sucked into a beam. For a second, everything is blurry. The next thing you know, you are in space. It is completely silent. Then you see a bunch of aliens. One alien is bigger than all the others. You think he must be the general alien. He is speaking a language you do not understand. The general alien notices that you do not understand him. He puts a device in your ear. Then you can hear his voice. It is very deep.
“What is your name?” the general asks.
“My name is Robert,” you say. All the aliens seem confused.
“Okay,” the general says. “I am going to call you Conner. My name is Frank.” Frank says that the ship is landing. While you are waiting, you look around and find out that you are on the mothership because there is a plate on the wall that says Mothership One.
When the big metal door opens, you see a whole parade. The aliens are celebrating because you are the first human to set foot on their planet. Frank says, “Go explore.”
On the left path, you see what looks like the Olympics, but alien style. On the right path, you see a variety of stores.

*label page8
You go right. One alien calls you over.
You walk up to the alien, and the alien gives you a small ship.
“I have always wanted to meet a human,” the alien says. “To honor you, I want you to have this ship.”
You take the ship for a test drive. At first, you feel nervous, but then you realize you are actually pretty good at flying it.
Then you get an idea.
You fly over to a fireworks shop. Since you are the first human on the planet, the aliens give you fireworks for free. You load them into your small ship and fly back toward the mothership.
You aim carefully, light the fireworks, and shoot them straight at the mothership.
BOOM!
The mothership explodes in the sky. You steer your small ship away from the blast and leave the alien planet behind.

“I am so glad to see you,” you say.
