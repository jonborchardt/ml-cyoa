You decide to save the mermaid. You begin your quest. You start swimming. You swim for two hours. Then you see a jellyfish. You think it will be easy to swim past it, so you go around it and then look up. You see an army of jellyfish! You touch one of them, and it glows pink before moving aside. You get an idea! You start feeling all of the heads of the jellyfish. The heads begin to glow red, orange, yellow, green, and blue, and even purple! They move aside, making a path. You swim through. You keep swimming. An hour has passed. You are really tired and hungry. Suddenly, you see a clam and open it to taste the most delicious meat you have ever had.  After a delicious meal of clams, you find a pair of dolphins. You all become friends, and they help you swim to the palace to rescue your mom. You find your mom and set her free. The two of you say goodbye to the dolphins and decide to go home. You arrive at the shore and return to being humans. You see your dad, and you all embrace in a loving hug.
