*title The Lost Signal
*author Anonymous

You are exploring your grandma's attic on a rainy afternoon when you find an old red walkie-talkie on a dusty shelf. As soon as you pick it up, it crackles to life.

"Hello? Is anyone there?" says a tiny, hopeful voice.

"Hello!" you say back. "Who are you?"

"My name is Pip," the voice says. "I'm a cloud sprite, and I'm lost. Will you help me find my way home?"

*choice
  #Ask Pip what they can see from where they are.
    "I'm near a very tall oak tree with a red swing," Pip says.
    *page_break
    You know that tree! It's at the park nearby. You grab your rain boots and run.
    *page_break
    At the park, you look up into the oak tree's branches. There, wedged in a fork between two limbs, is a tiny glowing creature no bigger than your fist.
    *page_break
    "Pip?" you whisper.
    *page_break
    The creature glows silver. "You came in person!"
    *page_break
    You cup your hands gently. Pip steps in — warm and feather-light. You lift your hands toward the sky, and a gust of wind carries Pip up, laughing all the way home.
    *finish
  #Tell Pip to follow the wind.
    "Which way is the wind blowing right now?" you ask.
    *page_break
    Pip goes quiet for a moment. "Northwest," Pip says. "My home is northwest — I can feel it!"
    *page_break
    "Then go that way," you say. "I'll watch until you disappear."
    *page_break
    A tiny silver light rises from somewhere over the rooftops, wobbles once, and zips northwest faster than a shooting star.
    *page_break
    The walkie-talkie gives one last crackle: "Found it! Thank you, ground-friend!"
    *finish
  #Suggest waiting until the moon comes out.
    "The moon will show you the way home once the sun sets," you say.
    *page_break
    "Then let's wait together," Pip says happily.
    *page_break
    You sit on a dusty trunk and talk all afternoon. Pip tells you about cloud cities and raindrop concerts. You tell Pip about school and grandma's cooking.
    *page_break
    When the moon finally rises, Pip gasps. "I can see the way! It was east all along."
    *page_break
    "Good luck, Pip," you say.
    *page_break
    "You are the best ground-friend a cloud sprite could ever find," Pip says softly.
    *finish
