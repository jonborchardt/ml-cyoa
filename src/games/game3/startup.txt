*title The Clockwork Garden
*author Anonymous

The garden smells like copper and rain. Every flower here is mechanical — gears for petals, springs for stems. A path of polished brass winds between the beds, clicking softly under your feet.

At the center of the garden stands a small copper fountain that spouts tiny golden sparks instead of water.

What would you like to explore first?

*choice
  #Follow the brass path deeper into the garden.
    The path leads past a row of mechanical sunflowers that slowly turn their gear-petal faces toward you as you walk by.
    *page_break
    At the end of the path is a small door built into a grassy hill, no taller than your knee. A sign above it reads: KEEPER'S COTTAGE.
    *page_break
    *choice
      #Knock on the tiny door.
        Three tiny taps. A long pause. Then the door swings open and a small mechanical owl steps out, blinking brass eyes.
        *page_break
        "Visitor!" the owl says, delighted. "I have not had a visitor in forty-seven years. Come — I will show you the garden's secret."
        *page_break
        The owl leads you behind the fountain to a hidden panel. Inside rests a single real green seed in a nest of tiny gears.
        *page_break
        "Every mechanical flower began as one of these," the owl says. "The first gardener planted it. The garden grew around it. We keep it safe."
        *page_break
        You look at the tiny seed and feel like you have been trusted with something important.
        *finish
      #Peek through the round window beside the door.
        Through the small window you see a cozy room — a miniature fireplace, a shelf of tiny books, and a rocking chair rocking all by itself.
        *page_break
        On the table is a cup of tea, still steaming.
        *page_break
        Someone — or something — lives here. You back away quietly, not wanting to disturb them.
        *page_break
        But as you turn to go, a tiny note slides under the door: "Thank you for not barging in. You are welcome back anytime."
        *finish
  #Pick one of the mechanical flowers.
    The stem resists at first, then releases with a soft click. The flower comes away cleanly, its petals spinning slowly in your hand.
    *page_break
    *choice
      #Wind the small key on the flower's stem.
        The flower hums. Its petals open wide, and inside the center is a tiny music box.
        *page_break
        It plays three notes — clear and sweet as bells — then goes quiet.
        *page_break
        You stand in the garden listening to the echo of the music until you cannot hear it anymore. You feel peaceful all the way down to your toes.
        *finish
      #Hold the flower up to the sunlight.
        Through the translucent gear-petals, the sun casts tiny spinning gear-shaped shadows on your palm.
        *page_break
        They turn slowly, like a clock.
        *page_break
        You watch until the shadows shift and the sun moves on. You feel like you could stay here forever.
        *finish
  #Sit beside the golden spark fountain.
    You sit on the fountain's edge and hold out your hand. A few sparks drift down and land on your palm — they are warm but do not burn, and they vanish after a moment with a soft tingle.
    *page_break
    You sit there a long time, catching sparks, while the mechanical flowers turn slowly in the breeze.
    *page_break
    When you finally stand to leave, every flower in the garden has turned to face you.
    *page_break
    A farewell. You wave back. It seems like the right thing to do.
    *finish
